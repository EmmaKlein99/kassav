What is Zouk?

Zouk is a French Caribbean music style that contains rhythm and beat, it makes you wanna dance and gives "feel-good" feeling. It was originally characterized by a fast tempo, a percussion-driven rhythm, two guitars and a loud horn section.

Kassav´ biography

Kassav' is a French Caribbean band formed in Guadeloupe in 1979. The core members of the band are Jacob Desvarieux, Jocelyne Béroard, Jean-Philippe Marthély, Patrick St. Eloi, Jean-Claude Naimro, Claude Vamur, and Georges Décimus (who left to form Volt Face and returned). Kassav' have issued over 20 albums, with a further 12 solo albums by band members.
